# Monetization Recommendation

## Primary recommendation

Sell **OpenClaw Launch Toolkit** as a **one-time paid bundle first**.

This is the cleanest fit for the current product shape because the offer is a finite package of practical documents and templates, not an always-on software subscription yet.

## Suggested pricing

- **$79** early launch / early buyer price
- **$129** standard launch price
- test **$149** after the documents are stronger and the page has better proof

## Recommended payment setup

Use the lowest-friction checkout option Steve is willing to wire up quickly:

1. **Gumroad** — simplest if digital download delivery matters most
2. **Lemon Squeezy** — strong option if Steve wants cleaner software/product commerce later
3. **Stripe Payment Link** — simplest if Steve just wants payment collection and manual delivery

## Why one-time paid first

- matches a document/toolkit product
- avoids pretending there is recurring value before it exists
- lowers buyer friction
- lets Steve validate willingness to pay immediately
- keeps the offer clearly productized instead of sounding like consulting

## Natural upsells

### Upsell 1: Updates Pack
A paid add-on or later subscription for:
- revised toolkit versions
- new examples
- additional templates
- future workflow packs

### Upsell 2: Implementation Session
A higher-ticket offer for buyers who want:
- a guided setup session
- personalized configuration help
- fast implementation of the toolkit in their environment

### Upsell 3: Premium Membership Later
Only after there is genuine ongoing value:
- recurring template drops
- advanced playbooks
- implementation office hours
- expanded OpenClaw operating packs

## Rule

Do **not** lead with consulting.
Lead with the toolkit product.
